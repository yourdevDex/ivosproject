IV of SPADES

IV of Spades is a Filipino rock band from Metro Manila, consisting of vocalist, bassist and keyboardist Zild Benitez, vocalist and lead guitarist Blaster Silonga, and drummer Badjao de Castro. The band first gained exposure with the release of their 4th single "Mundo".

Their musical style has been described as "pop funk" and funk rock by the local press. This band has gained so much popularity for its great music, and for the members' fashion style. Their fashion style tends to be highly influenced by '70s outfits.
